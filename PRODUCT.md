# FoodSaver — Product Context

## Product Purpose
FoodSaver is a food surplus marketplace connecting Malaysian food businesses (vendors) with budget-conscious buyers, reducing food waste while making affordable meals accessible. Built as a student project for BIIT 2305 (Web Application Development).

## Users
- **Vendors**: Food business operators (cafes, stalls, restaurants) who post surplus food listings with price, quantity, and pickup time.
- **Buyers**: Individuals looking for affordable food; browse, filter, and reserve listings.
- **Admins**: Platform moderators who manage users, listings, and content quality.

## Register
Mixed surface:
- `brand` — welcome/marketing page, auth pages (login/register): design IS the product; first impression for new visitors.
- `product` — vendor dashboard (listings CRUD), buyer marketplace (browse/reserve), admin dashboard (users/listings management): design SERVES the task.

## Brand Voice
Warm, communal, purposeful. A neighborhood marketplace that takes food waste seriously without being preachy. Direct and approachable — like a well-run pasar malam (Malaysian night market) with clear signage.

## Brand Colors
Forest green (primary surface/hero), amber (accent/CTA), warm cream (page background). These carry the "fresh produce, sustainable" identity without being generic eco-green.

## Anti-References
- Do not look like a generic food delivery app (Grab Food, FoodPanda aesthetic)
- Do not look like a corporate sustainability report
- Do not look like a social network

## Platform
Web only. Desktop-primary for vendors and admins; mobile-capable for buyers browsing listings.

## Tone
Warm but efficient. Copy is direct — no filler sentences. Role-specific surfaces feel purpose-built, not generic.
